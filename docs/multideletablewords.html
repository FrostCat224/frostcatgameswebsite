Multideletable Words

Multideletable words are words where you can remove any letter and it stays a word. For example: boats. You can remove the o from boats and it will result in bats, which is a real word. Removing the b will result in oats. You can do this for any letter. Oats, bats, bots, boas and boat. I got inspired by a now deleted Reddit post in r/theydidthemath, where someone asked what the longest word that can do that is.

Here are some lists. (words are counted as real in these lists if they exist in https://github.com/dwyl/english-words/blob/master/words.txt)
