Find the Breadsellers

Find the Breadsellers is a roblox game I made. The goal is, of course, to find Breadsellers. Breadsellers are dinosaur plushies that sell bread. The game currently has 221 (might be outdated) Breadsellers to find!

You can play it here!

The game is inspired by various other Find the ... games and by Flovis' most wonderful, most fat plushie ever, Broodjesverkoper (or Breadseller in english)

The game wouldn't become so big without Flovis, his alt account penguin_turtle1234, kp, MrArma and Pups la Rommelkoe. Because they motivated me and played my game!

Some Breadsellers are VERY VERY hard to get. For example Galaxy Breadseller, Breadseller Studio and Qwerty Breadseller. Some Breadsellers are very very EASY to get. For example Spawn Breadseller, the normal Breadseller and Psychic Breadseller.

You should definitely play FTBS! :)
